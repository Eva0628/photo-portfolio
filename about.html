About

I'm an amateur photographer with a particular interest in travel, wildlife, and portrait photography. I shoot across a mix of gear — a Panasonic ZS99 for travel and wildlife, a Nikon D750 for portraits, and my Honor 30s phone for whatever moment can't wait for a professional camera.

Photography and front-end design share the same instinct for me: noticing detail, composition, and how light or space guides attention — which is part of why I enjoy both.
